## Usage Tips

### Adapting These Prompts

1. **Replace specific elements:**
   - Change colors: "teal coupe" → "red sports car"
   - Change time of day: "morning" → "golden hour evening"
   - Change setting: "modern office" → "cozy home workspace"

2. **Adjust camera movement:**
   - "slow pan right" → "slow pan left" or "static shot"
   - "dolly forward" → "zoom in" or "static shot"
   - "tracking shot" → "static wide shot"

3. **Modify lighting:**
   - "natural window light" → "soft studio lighting"
   - "golden hour" → "blue hour" or "noon sun"
   - "dramatic lighting" → "soft even lighting"

4. **Change duration/resolution:**
   - 5s for quick cuts and simple scenes
   - 10s for establishing shots and complex scenes
   - 720p for social media and iteration
   - 1080p for production and detailed shots

### Combining Elements

Mix elements from different prompts:

**Base:** Coffee Pour prompt
**+ Camera from:** Geometric Patterns ("slow camera rotation")
**+ Lighting from:** Product Shot ("soft studio lighting from above")

**Result:**
```
Close-up of fresh coffee being poured into white ceramic mug on wooden table,
steam rising, soft studio lighting from above creating subtle shadows, slow
camera rotation around subject
```

### Testing Workflow

1. Start with example prompt as-is
2. Generate with sora-2 at 720p
3. Review result
4. Make ONE specific change (lighting, camera, subject)
5. Regenerate or use remix
6. Repeat until satisfied
7. Final render with sora-2-pro if needed
